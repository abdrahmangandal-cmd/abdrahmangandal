Research & Projects

Augmented Solow Growth Model

Extension of the standard Solow model incorporating capital, labour-augmenting technology, and a fixed production factor. Work includes formal derivation of the balanced growth path and phase diagram analysis of steady-state dynamics following a change in the savings rate.
